	1 & 2 & 3\\

## Systèmes linéaires

1. $$\left\{\begin{array}{rrrl}
   3x &+ y &- 4z &= 12\\
   & 2y &-  z&= -1\\
   &&3z &= 6
   \end{array}\right.$$,

2. $$\left\{\begin{array}{rrrl}
   x &+ y &+ z &= 3\\
   2x &+ 7y &- 2z &= -1\\
   -x &- y &+ z &= 3
   \end{array}\right.$$,

3. $$\left\{\begin{array}{rrrrl}
   t &-3x &+ y &+ 2z &= -2\\
     &2x &+ 3y &- 7z &= 4\\
   4t &&+   5y &+ z  &= 10\\
   \end{array}\right.$$,

   x &+ y &+ 2z &= 4\\
